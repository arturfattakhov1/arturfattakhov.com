# ADR: Consultation MVP boundaries

Status: accepted for Slice 6.

## Decision

- Use an application-first flow: application, preliminary review, individual agreement of format and price, full payment by an agreed method, then consultation.
- Reuse the existing Formspree endpoint as the only form backend.
- Do not add website payment, checkout, uploads, automatic scheduling, or invoice automation.
- Do not present remote work as a definitive diagnosis or a replacement for physical examination.
- Do not accept emergency use; direct urgent conditions to the nearest available veterinary clinic, preferably one open around the clock.
- Treat the application as a request for review, not automatic case acceptance or a confirmed consultation.
- Agree the format and price individually before payment.

## Boundaries and review trigger

Public legal copy requires professional legal review before expanding payments, international availability or medical-file processing.

Revisit this decision only after owner approval and professional legal review for any expansion of geography, payment processing, medical-file handling, or clinical scope.
